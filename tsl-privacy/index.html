By using GlucoGuide, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy. GlucoGuide reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Information We Collect

We collect both personally identifiable information (PII) and non-personally identifiable information (non-PII):

  • Personal Information: Information you provide voluntarily, such as your name, email, address, payment details, and data submitted during registration or purchase.
  • Automatic Data Collection: Information such as your device’s IP address, browser type, and usage data, which helps us enhance your experience and monitor app performance. This data is collected via cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect allows us to:

  • Process orders and manage your account.
  • Improve the functionality and experience of the app and website.
  • Provide personalized recommendations and offers based on your preferences.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, and relevant health insights.
  • Deliver targeted ads through various channels, such as email or mobile notifications.

You may opt out of receiving promotional communications at any time by following the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or by contacting our support team.

We use cookies (small data files) and web beacons (clear images embedded in our app or emails) to track interactions, enhance user experience, and collect browsing information. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Although GlucoGuide primarily serves users in the U.S., we respect the rights of users under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • CCPA: California residents may request access to or deletion of their personal information or opt out of data sharing. Reach out to appglucoguide@outlook.com for such requests.
